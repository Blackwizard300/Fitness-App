Shoulders Workout

1. Overhead Barbell Press – 4 sets x 6-8

  • Heavy compound movement
  • Keep your core tight and avoid over-arching

2. Dumbbell Lateral Raises – 4 sets x 12-15

  • Slow and controlled
  • Slight lean forward, lead with your elbows

3. Arnold Press – 3 sets x 10-12

  • Great for front delts and overall shape
  • Rotate through the motion for full range

4. Cable Rear Delt Fly – 3 sets x 12-15

  • Focus on rear delts — don't let traps take over
  • Can do bent-over dumbbell reverse flys instead

5. Upright Rows – 3 sets x 10-12

  • Hits traps and side delts
  • Keep elbows above wrists

6. Face Pulls – 3 sets x 15

  • Posterior chain + rotator cuff health
  • Pull to your nose/eyes with rope
