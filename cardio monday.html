HIIT Cardio Blast

1. Warm-Up (10 min)

  • Arm circles, jumping jacks, high knees, bodyweight squats

2. HIIT Circuit (1 hr 15 min) – Repeat 3x:

  • 45 sec burpees → 15 sec rest
  • 45 sec jump squats → 15 sec rest
  • 45 sec mountain climbers → 15 sec rest
  • 45 sec skater hops → 15 sec rest
  • 1 min rest between rounds

3. Core + Cooldown (35 min)

  • Bicycle crunches: 3 x 30
  • Side planks: 3 x 30 sec/side
  • Stretch: Quads, glutes, hamstrings
