Chest Day

1. Barbell Bench Press – 4 sets x 6-8 reps

  • Classic for building power and size
  • Focus on control, touch chest lightly

2. Incline Dumbbell Press – 4 sets x 8-10 reps

  • Emphasizes upper chest
  • Keep the angle around 30–45 degrees

3. Cable or Machine Chest Fly – 3 sets x 12-15 reps

  • Great stretch and contraction
  • Squeeze at the center, slow on the way back

4. Dips (Chest-focused) – 3 sets x 8-12 reps

  • Lean forward slightly to hit lower chest
  • Add weight if body weight gets too easy

5. Push-Ups (Drop Set) – 3 sets to failure

  • Start with normal push-ups, then knees if needed
  • Burnout for volume
