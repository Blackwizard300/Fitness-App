Plyometrics + Jump Rope

1. Plyometric Training (45 min)

  • Box jumps: 3 x 10
  • Jump lunges: 3 x 15/leg
  • Tuck jumps: 3 x 10

2. Jump Rope Intervals (45 min)

  • 1 min fast jump → 30 sec rest → repeat for 8–10 rounds
  • Try double-unders or side-to-side hops for challenge

3. Mobility + Stretch (30 min)

  • Hip openers, foam rolling, shoulder mobility
