Cardio & Abs Workout

Cardio

  • Jumping Jacks / Jump Rope
  • Mountain Climbers
  • Plank-to-Elbow Taps

  • Russian Twists (weighted if you can)
  • Burpee or Squat Jumps
  • Leg Raises or Hanging Leg Raises

ABS

  • Flutter Kicks – 30 sec
  • Side Plank (each side) – 30 sec
  • Toe Touch Crunches – 20 reps
