Legs Workout

1. Barbell Back Squats – 4 sets x 6-8

  • King of leg day
  • Go deep, brace the core, drive through heels

2. Romanian Deadlifts (RDLs) – 4 sets x 8-10

  • Focus on hamstring stretch and glute squeeze
  • Hinge at the hips, keep the bar close to shins

3. Walking Lunges (DB or BB) – 3 sets x 12-14 steps per leg

  • Glutes and quads, plus balance and coordination
  • Keep chest up and take long strides

4. Leg Press (or Hack Squat) – 3 sets x 10-12

  • Stack some volume here
  • Don't lock your knees at the top

5. Leg Curls (Seated or Lying) – 3 sets x 12-15

  • Focus on squeezing the hamstrings

6. Standing or Seated Calf Raises – 4 sets x 15-20

  • Full stretch and strong contraction
  • Slow, controlled tempo for growth
