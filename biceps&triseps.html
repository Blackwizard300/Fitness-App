Biceps & triceps workout

Superset 1

  • Barbell Curls – 4 sets x 8-10
  • Close-Grip Bench Press – 4 sets x 8-10Heavy compound for both arms

Superset 2

  • Incline Dumbbell Curls – 3 sets x 10-12
  • Overhead Dumbbell Triceps Extensions – 3 sets x 10-12Stretch and isolate both heads fully

Superset 3

  • Hammer Curls (Rope or Dumbbell) – 3 sets x 12-15
  • Triceps Pushdown (Rope or Bar) – 3 sets x 12-15Great for forearms and triceps detail

Finisher

  • 21s (Biceps curls) – 2 sets(7 bottom-half reps, 7 top-half, 7 full)
  • Bench Dips – 2 sets to failure
