Back workout

1. Deadlifts – 4 sets x 6-8 reps

  • Builds overall posterior strength
  • Focus on form, hinge from the hips

2. Pull-Ups / Lat Pulldowns – 4 sets x 8-10 reps

  • Wide grip for upper lat focus
  • Use assistance if needed

3. Barbell or Dumbbell Bent Over Rows – 4 sets x 8-10 reps

  • Pull to the waist, squeeze the shoulder blades

4. Single-Arm Dumbbell Rows – 3 sets x 10-12 reps (each arm)

  • Focus on full range of motion

5. Face Pulls (Cable or Bands) – 3 sets x 12-15 reps

  • Great for rear delts and traps

6. Hyperextensions or Reverse Hypers – 3 sets x 15 reps

  • Targets lower back and glutes
